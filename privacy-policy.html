Privacy Policy for Prosper Budget Planner

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Prosper Budget Planner ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account using Google Sign-In, we receive your email address and display name from Google.
  • Financial Data: Budget categories, transactions, income, expenses, and savings goals you enter into the app.
  • Preferences: Your chosen currency, spending limits, and app settings.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the budget tracking functionality
  • To sync your data across devices when signed in
  • To process premium subscription purchases
  • To send important notifications about your account or spending alerts
  • To improve and optimize our app

Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using secure database encryption. When you sign in with Google, your account information is managed through Firebase Authentication, which uses industry-standard security practices.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication: For secure user authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your financial data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Account information is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data (available in Premium)
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment information. Subscription management and billing are handled entirely by Google Play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
